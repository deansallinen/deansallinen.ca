About me

I am a designer from Vancouver BC currently focused on User Experience and User Interface.

I come from an academic background in Psychology. Studying human behaviour has given me invaluable insights, along with research and interviewing skills which I use in my design process.

My work history spans industries and job titles and I see my varied experience as a benefit when designing for end users.

When I’m not working on my design projects I enjoy learning new songs on guitar, discovering new Celiac friendly foods, and playing with my adopted rabbit Max.

Education

  • Simon Fraser University, B.A. Psychology
  • Simon Fraser University, Certificate of Liberal Arts
